--- title: IDC1 ---

The International Department of Citizenship is an organizing body dedicated to empowering citizen projects which lay the groundwork for revolution and allow workers to control the means of production in a democratic & decentralized world.


Our goal is to organize and coordinate actions which challenge capitalism, injustice, and the centers of power most susceptible to corruption.


We aim to create a central confederation which facilitates a network of fully independent regional mutual aid groups.


IDC1 is the online assembly of the IDC. We create and manage digital tools and resources, with a goal of establishing a communications infrastructure to support and empower citizen movements.


We operate as a collective, meaning we have a flat structure without any bosses or orders. You’re free to set your own schedule and create your own projects to invite others to, but we have lots objectives in need of help if you’re just looking to get started with collaborating ASAP.

We take a goal-oriented approach to leftist organizing. Yes, reading and theory are good, but we ensure regular completion of objectives in a collaborative, action focused environment.


We are a flat collective, so there are no bosses, but if you're not sure of what project you'd like to start, we encourage channel building to broaden awareness of society's issues and their root cause.
